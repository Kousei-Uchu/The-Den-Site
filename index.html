About The Den

A welcoming space for furries of all walks of life to express themselves, share their passions, and connect with potential new friends.

Community

Join our friendly, active community. Founded in 2024, we're a safe space for everyfur. We have daily questions, weekly events, and special activities to make sure there's always something to look forward to! Our members range from artists and writers to gamers and fursuiters.

Creativity

Showcase your art, writing, or fursuits in our dedicated channels. We have #art-showcase, #writing-corner for stories and poetry, and #fursuit-friday for suiters. Get friendly feedback from other members and participate in the event canvases.

Inclusivity

We welcome furries of all backgrounds, diagnoses, identities, and experience levels. Our mod team ensures a safe and welcoming space for everyone.

Community Rules

To keep The Den a safe and welcoming space, we ask all members to follow these simple rules.

1. Be Respectful

Treat all members with kindness and respect. No harassment, discrimination, or hate speech of any kind.

2. Keep it SFW

Keep all content appropriate for all ages. NSFW content must go in designated 18+ channels with proper tagging.

3. No Spam

Avoid excessive messaging, pinging, or self-promotion. Use appropriate channels for all content.

4. Respect Privacy

Do not share personal information about yourself or others without consent.

5. Follow Discord TOS

All Discord Community Guidelines must be followed at all times.

6. Have Fun!

Most importantly, relax, be yourself, and enjoy being part of our community!

Breaking these rules may result in warnings, mutes, or bans at moderator discretion.

About The Den

A welcoming space for furries of all walks of life to express themselves, share their passions, and connect with potential new friends.

Community

Join our friendly, active community. Founded in 2024, we're a safe space for everyfur. We have daily questions, weekly events, and special activities to make sure there's always something to look forward to! Our members range from artists and writers to gamers and fursuiters.

Creativity

Showcase your art, writing, or fursuits in our dedicated channels. We have #art-showcase, #writing-corner for stories and poetry, and #fursuit-friday for suiters. Get friendly feedback from other members and participate in the event canvases.

Inclusivity

We welcome furries of all backgrounds, diagnoses, identities, and experience levels. Our mod team ensures a safe and welcoming space for everyone.

Community Rules

To keep The Den a safe and welcoming space, we ask all members to follow these simple rules.

1. Be Respectful

Treat all members with kindness and respect. No harassment, discrimination, or hate speech of any kind.

2. Keep it SFW

Keep all content appropriate for all ages over 13. NSFW content must go elsewhere as we have minors.

3. No Spam

Avoid excessive messaging, pinging, or self-promotion. Use appropriate channels for all content.

4. Respect Privacy

Do not share personal information about yourself or others without consent the individual and the green light from staff.

5. Follow Discord TOS

All Discord Community Guidelines must be followed at all times.

6. Sensitive Topics

Some topics are limited to designated channels. A list of these topics can be found HERE. Please read over them.

7. No Malicious Content

No grabify links, viruses, crash videos, links to viruses, or token grabbers. These will result in an immidiate ban.

8. Media Spoilers

Be considerate and don't spoil things for others, mark it and label it as a spoiler or better still keep it to DMs

9. Use English Only

It makes moderating much harder, and it excludes others who do not know these languages. Some things are excepted, but assume not.

10. Do Not DM Staff

Causal chats in DMs are ok (as per their preference role), but all server related discussion or debates should happen in a ticket. This is here for a reason.

11. Accounts

Everything shown on your Discord Profile, or any other accounts you share here must follow these rules. Alternate accounts are not allowed.

12. Common Sense

These rules are not all-encompassing, please use your common sense. If you're not sure if something breaks a rule or if it is borderline, check in a ticket first.

Breaking these rules may result in warnings, mutes, or bans at moderator discretion. If you have any questions, please ask. If a staff member is acting unfairly, please let us know.
